Project Word Association

This is a project for IFN701 Project 1 at Queensland University of Technology. The original objective was to measure distances between synonyms. However, during the course of the project, we realised that synonymy is not enough to capture all the associations between words. Therefore, we developed more functionalities, namely related words and related concepts, to return more possible associations for a word given by the user.

More information can be found in the Project Plan
